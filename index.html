// WHO

We race bikes.
We drink coffee.
We eat bars.
We do it again.

COSDI is a loose gang of eleven people held together by lycra, group chat memes, and an unreasonable love for getting up early on Saturday. We show up at the start line from Barry-Roubaix to Iceman, turn pedals in circles, and then go get tacos.

We are not pros. We are definitely committed.
