// WHO

We go in circles.
We drink coffee.
We yell a lot.
We do it again.

COSDI is a loose gang of eleven people held together by lycra, group chat memes, and an unreasonable love for pinning on race numbers. We show up at crits across the Midwest, go in circles for an hour, argue about the finish line sprint, and then go get tacos.

We are not pros. We are definitely committed.
